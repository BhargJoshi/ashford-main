Got questions? We've got answers.

What trading experience do I need to join?

Our programs cater to all levels, from absolute beginners to intermediate traders looking to refine their strategy. We focus on building fundamental precision first.

How is the mentorship delivered?

Mentorship is delivered through a combination of live weekly webinars, recorded video modules, direct one-on-one strategy sessions, and a private community chat for real-time support.

What markets do you cover?

Our core principles are universally applicable. While we primarily use Indian equity and commodity markets for examples, the techniques can be applied to Forex, Crypto, and US Stocks.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a conditional 7-day refund policy, provided the student has completed less than 10% of the core content and provides a valid reason. Please read the full terms before enrolling.

Can you make me rich overnight?

No. Trading is a skill that requires discipline, practice, and time. We focus on consistent, sustainable growth, not quick predictions or high-risk gambling.
